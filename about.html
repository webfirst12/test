About

Manipur Medicinal Plants is a catalogue of the medicinal plants in Manipur. It is an attempt to provide information about the various herbs of Manipur to the general public.

This resource aims to serve as a comprehensive guide for students, and the general public interested in the traditional and medicinal uses of Manipur's flora.

Manipur Medicinal Plants is a tool for anyone interested in the field of ethnobotany, natural medicine, and conservation. By bridging the gap between traditional knowledge and modern science, we hope to promote the sustainable use of these medicinal plants and contribute to the health and well-being of communities in Manipur and beyond.
